CORE PRINCIPLES OF QAZLIKE
Technological Democracy (Democracy 2.0) is a new form of people-powered governance where key state functions are implemented through technology: blockchain, artificial intelligence, digital voting, and smart contracts.
The core principle is power to the people, because the money is with the people.
The Essence:
This is the evolution of democracy, a transition from a representative form (where power is delegated) to a technological one (where the people govern directly).


I. PARTICIPATORY BUDGETING 2.0: FINANCIAL SOVEREIGNTY THROUGH DIRECT DISTRIBUTION
The key principle that distinguishes Technological Democracy from all previous systems is that 100% of state revenues are first deposited into the personal digital wallets of citizens. This is not just a payment mechanism, but a fundamental mechanism for protecting democracy.
Why is this the only correct model? Because Money = Power.
If funds were frozen in a general state account before distribution, it would replicate the old model where the bureaucracy retains control over finances—and thus, real power. In the QazLike model, this loophole for corruption, manipulation, and lobbying is eliminated at its root: from the moment revenues are received, the citizens-shareholders become their sole managers. This transforms democracy from a ritual of voting every few years into a continuous, technology-protected process of governance, where you don't just "take their word for it," but physically see and control your share of the national wealth.


II. THREE TIERS OF GOVERNANCE: A NEW ARCHITECTURE OF RESPONSIBILITY AND FREEDOM
To ensure the system is not only fair but also secure, a clear hierarchy for fund management is introduced, guaranteeing that society's basic needs are met first.
First – Tier 1 (Strategic Framework): Security and Justice.
Citizens collectively vote to fund defense, national security, and the judiciary. Expert groups are created to inform citizens. Once approved by a majority, the required sum is automatically deducted from every wallet into the protected Tier 1 pool. This guarantees that the foundation of the state is unshakable.
Then – Tier 2 (Development and Functioning): Effective Government.
After security is ensured, citizens vote for a development package covering critical infrastructure, fundamental science, national roads, energy grids, and support for strategic innovations (e.g., a national AI supercomputer). Expert groups are created to inform citizens. The approved sum is also automatically deducted into the Tier 2 pool, from which projects are funded, implemented by a government that operates on a competitive basis with KPIs.
And Only Then – Tier 3 (Personal and Collective Sovereignty): Your Economic Freedom.
The funds remaining in a citizen's wallet after financing Tiers 1 and 2 come under their full and unconditional control. These funds can only be managed after the first two tiers have been approved and paid for. The citizen can manage this money personally—spend it on healthcare, education, consumption, or invest in projects through a crowd-investing platform, becoming a co-owner of the future.
Why is this order mandatory? It is a hierarchy of rational governance, built into the system's code. First, security is collectively ensured, then development, and only after that does the phase of personal economic freedom begin. This is a technological guarantee that your personal freedom is built on a solid foundation of collective security and rational development. The algorithm makes this process irreversible and transparent, eliminating the very possibility of manipulating public funds.
The Final Message:
"We are not breaking your democracy. We are upgrading it. We take your core values—freedom, market, control over power—and give you the technological tools to realize them 100%. QazLike is Democracy 2.0: fair, transparent, and truly belonging to you."


III. COMPETITIVE FORMATION OF THE GOVERNMENT: PERFORMANCE-BASED GOVERNANCE
The Problem:
In traditional systems, governments are formed based on political deals, party loyalties, or the will of a narrow elite. There is no direct link between the government's performance and the economic well-being of the people. Bureaucracy becomes self-serving, and citizens have no real mechanism to hold the entire executive branch accountable for results.
The QazLike Solution:
QazLike replaces political appointment with a transparent, competitive, and merit-based selection process for the entire executive branch—the Government.
Open National Competition: The role of the Government is put out to a national tender. Competing teams (including a candidate for Prime Minister and key ministers) present their detailed strategic programs and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) on the QazLike platform.
Informed Citizen Choice: All citizens and independent expert groups can study, compare, and debate the teams' programs directly on the platform. This ensures an informed and transparent selection process.
A Mandate for Results: The team with the best strategic vision and the most convincing plan for national development, as chosen by a majority in a national digital vote, receives the mandate to govern.
KPI for Power: A Focus on Growing People's Incomes
The core incentive for the governing team is directly tied to the prosperity of the citizens.
The Ultimate KPI: The primary performance indicator for the government is the growth of the population's income. This is measured by the increase in the total sum of funds remaining on citizens' personal wallets (Tier 3) after financing Tiers 1 and 2. A successful government is one that enriches the people.
Performance-Based Earnings: The governing team earns a pre-defined percentage from the GROWTH of state revenues. If they manage the economy and national assets effectively, leading to an increase in the total state income, they receive a bonus. The more the budget grows, the more the team earns.
Automatic Accountability: This system is enforced by smart contracts. If the KPIs for state revenue growth are not met, bonus payments are automatically blocked. Consistent failure leads to the team's dismissal and a new national competition.
The Result:
True Accountability: The government's interests are perfectly aligned with those of the people. Their success is measured by the citizens' financial well-being.
Meritocracy over Politics: The best and most competent teams with proven strategies are incentivized to lead, not the most politically connected ones.
An End to Stagnation: The government is motivated to foster a dynamic, innovative, and prosperous economy that benefits everyone, as their own reward depends on it.
This model transforms the government from a bureaucratic administrator into a high-performance management team, directly accountable to its shareholders—the citizens.

QazLike introduces a new kind of freedom – The Consumer Revolution in Public Services.
We don't just promise better healthcare or education. We create them through real market competition and technological transparency.
Imagine this:
Instead of paying taxes into a bureaucratic system, you receive a direct digital dividend.
You become a powerful, financially empowered citizen.
For Healthcare
You choose any private clinic directly.
No more insurance networks deciding which doctor you can see.
Competition for your funding raises quality and lowers prices – because you hold the power.
For Education
Your child's education fund grows from birth.
You choose the best schools and universities, not the ones tied to your postcode or your debt.
Institutions compete for your trust – not for bureaucracy's approval.
The role of the state evolves – from being an inefficient manager of hospitals and schools to becoming a guarantor of your financial ability to choose.
This is true consumer sovereignty – a market that finally works for citizens, not for institutions.
QazLike turns freedom, fairness, and technology into one living system – a Technological Democracy where choice replaces corruption and transparency replaces control.


V. THE ABOLITION OF ELECTED REPRESENTATIVES: DIRECT DEMOCRACY INSTEAD OF REPRESENTATIVE ILLUSION
The Problem.
Traditional democracies are based on the institution of elected representatives (deputies, senators), who are supposed to express the will of the people.
Over time, however, they transform into a closed political class, detached from citizens. Their activity concentrates not on solving public tasks, but on partisan games, lobbying, and serving the interests of donors.
The average person delegates all their power for 4-5 years—and loses real influence over decisions.
The QazLike Solution.
Technological democracy makes political intermediaries obsolete.
QazLike completely abolishes the institution of deputyship.
Its functions are transferred to the citizens themselves via a digital platform, blockchain, and artificial intelligence.
The Result.
Real, not ritual, power.
Citizens make decisions directly, daily, not once every few years in a voting booth.
The disappearance of the political class.
No more deputies with privileges, status, and salaries for "thinking for the people."
The end of lobbying.
It is impossible to bribe millions of citizens making collective decisions.
Corporations lose their monopoly on influence, and the state becomes a truly people's instrument.
QazLike does not destroy democracy—it completes its evolution.
Power returns to its only legitimate source—the citizen.
This is Democracy 2.0—a technological democracy where every citizen becomes a full participant in governing their country.


VI. A LIVING DIGITAL CONSTITUTION – THE FUNDAMENTAL LAW THAT EVOLVES WITH SOCIETY
The Problem.
A traditional Constitution is a static document, almost immovable in time.
The process of amending it takes years and depends on the will of the political class.
As a result, the Fundamental Law lags behind the development of technology, society, and the economy, losing relevance and connection to reality.
The QazLike Solution.
We transfer the Constitution into digital space, turning it into a living and evolving system—a Living Digital Constitution.
Popular sovereignty in action.
Every citizen can propose an amendment.
Upon reaching a set level of support (e.g., 500,000 signatures), it is automatically put to a national digital vote.
Transparency and immutability.
All versions of the Constitution, all proposals, and every vote are recorded on the blockchain, ensuring immutability and full verifiability.
The history of the constitutional process becomes open to all citizens.
Relevance and legitimacy.
The Constitution ceases to be a frozen monument of the past.
It becomes a flexible, living instrument that quickly adapts to modern challenges—from artificial intelligence and cybersecurity to ecology and digital rights.
Every citizen becomes a co-author of the Fundamental Law, making it maximally legitimate.
The Result for Citizens:
You are a co-author of the Constitution.
Your vote directly influences the formation of the country's Fundamental Law.
Protection from abuse.
No party, group, or elite can change the rules of the game in their favor.
Farewell to legal stagnation.
Values and laws are synchronized with reality. The Constitution always reflects the spirit of the time and the will of society, not the interests of the ruling class.
The Living Digital Constitution is not just text on a website.
It is a dynamic social contract, written and updated by the people in real time.
It guarantees that power remains with the citizens, not the politicians.
Thus, QazLike transforms the Constitution from a symbol of the past into a technology of the future.


VII. FAIR DISTRIBUTION OF NATIONAL AND DIGITAL WEALTH
The Problem:
In the 21st century, national wealth is not only oil, gas, and minerals.
It is also energy, data, artificial intelligence, and digital infrastructure.
However, control over these resources is concentrated in the hands of corporations and narrow elites, not the citizens to whom they rightfully belong.
You do not own the data you create daily, nor do you receive a share of the profit generated by national AI systems and digital platforms.
The QazLike Solution:
QazLike introduces a universal principle of fair distribution for all forms of national wealth—
be it natural resources, energy, telecommunications spectrum, revenues from national AI systems, or aggregated anonymous data.
Every citizen receives their share of these revenues in their personal electronic wallet via automated blockchain smart contracts.
This transforms every citizen from a passive "taxpayer" into a shareholder of the country—a co-owner of natural, digital, and intellectual assets.
The Result for Citizens:
Systemic inequality disappears. Profit from natural and digital resources is distributed among all citizens, not concentrated in corporate accounts.
Digital sovereignty. Everyone receives an economic benefit from the technologies and data created in their territory and with their participation.
Justice as the basis of the economy. A new model emerges—an economy of shared ownership and collective prosperity, where technological progress serves the well-being of all, not the enrichment of a few.
The Role of the State:
The state ceases to be the monopolistic owner of national assets.
It becomes a transparent administrator, managing resources by direct mandate and under the control of the society-owner.


VIII. THE TECHNOLOGICAL FOUNDATION OF QAZLIKE: BLOCKCHAIN AND SMART CONTRACTS – THE SKELETON OF A NEW DEMOCRACY
For the first time in millennia of human history, technology allows for the realization of the ideal of complete people-powered governance.
The Technological Democracy of QazLike makes every citizen not just a voter, but a direct participant in governance, possessing 100% power over their state.
This revolution is made possible by three technological pillars:
1. Blockchain – The Backbone of Absolute Transparency
The entire state budget, all revenues and expenditures, are recorded in a public, immutable, and decentralized ledger.
Every transaction—from billion-dollar contracts to dividend payments—is visible to all and permanently stored in the system.
This technologically excludes corruption, manipulation, and concealment of information.
You no longer believe reports—you see the movement of every unit of the budget in real time.
2. Smart Contracts – The Muscles Executing the Will of the People
A smart contract is a piece of program code that automatically executes decisions approved by citizens in a vote.
Tier 1 budget approved? Funds are automatically reserved and controlled.
Government KPIs met? Bonuses are paid automatically. Not met—payments are blocked.
A project raised the required sum through crowd-investing? Shares are distributed instantly.
The human factor and corruption become technically impossible: the system cannot "change its mind" or "make a deal."
3. Supercomputers and Digital Infrastructure – The Nervous System of Governance
Processing millions of transactions, modeling the consequences of decisions, cryptographic data protection, and the stable operation of the platform for tens of millions of users—all this is ensured by the computing power of supercomputers and artificial intelligence.
They make direct democracy scalable, secure, and fast.
The Bottom Line
Power finally belongs to the people—not in words, but at the level of computer code.
Blockchain guarantees transparency.
Smart contracts guarantee the execution of decisions.
Supercomputers guarantee system stability.
This is the operating system for a free society.
This is Democracy 2.0.


IX. DIGITAL RESPONSIBILITY: THE INSEPARABILITY OF RIGHTS AND DUTIES
In the QazLike system, freedom and justice are two sides of the same coin.
Upon receiving new rights—a share of national wealth and direct participation in governance—every citizen also assumes digital responsibility.
This principle is not an external requirement but a built-in function of the system itself, ensured by smart contracts operating with the impartiality of a machine.
Automated Justice
Any violation confirmed by the system (be it a court sentence or blockchain-recorded fraud) triggers automatic and inevitable consequences, pre-defined in the smart contracts.
For Citizens:
Evasion of public duties or serious offenses leads to fines;
Potential temporary exclusion from the distribution of the national dividend;
And in extreme cases—to the lifelong deprivation of this share.
For Officials:
Failure to meet KPIs, abuse of power, or confirmed violations automatically entail:
Dismissal from their position;
Financial sanctions, including all measures applied to citizens (fines, temporary exclusion from the national dividend, and in severe cases—lifelong deprivation of the share);
A lifelong ban on working in government bodies.
Why It Works
Fairness. Responsibility is proportional to authority: the higher the position, the stricter the consequences.
Transparency. All rules are public and the same for everyone. The algorithm excludes backroom deals and selectivity.
Deterrence. The awareness of the automation and inevitability of punishment becomes the most powerful barrier to corruption and abuse.
The Bottom Line
This is a technological balance between freedom and responsibility.
A system where being honest is not only a moral choice but also a rational economic calculation.
For the first time, justice ceases to depend on the human factor and becomes part of the very architecture of democracy.

In its place, faith in justice is restored.

The need to survive disappears—the opportunity to live and develop appears.
Social Aggression. When glaring inequality disappears, the fuel for envy, hatred, and mutual enmity is removed.
Society transitions from a state of confrontation to a state of cooperation and mutual trust.
Crime. With the disappearance of poverty, injustice, and corruption, the very motivation to break the law vanishes.
Crime ceases to be a means of survival or compensation for social grievance.
Justice, transparency, and collective responsibility create an environment where breaking the rules becomes pointless and unprofitable.
The Flourishing of Human Potential
The struggle for survival is replaced by an era of creation.
A colossal amount of energy, previously spent on anxiety, fear, and anger, is freed up.
Creativity. People gain the internal freedom to take risks, change professions, engage in art, science, and innovation—without the fear of falling below the poverty line.
Family. Economic stability restores the meaning of love and parenthood.
People start families based on desire, not on calculation or fear.
The number of abortions and child abandonments decreases—the very economic pressure forcing such choices disappears.
Health and Psyche. Chronic stress, depression, and addiction recede.
A healthier, more resilient, and happier society is formed.
The Bottom Line
QazLike is not just an operating system for a new economy.
It is a system of psychological, moral, and social regeneration for society.
It changes not individuals, but the very environment in which they live, creating conditions where honesty, trust, kindness, and creativity become natural forms of life.


XI. A UNIVERSAL PATH FOR ALL NATIONS: EVOLUTION INSTEAD OF REVOLUTION
QazLike is not a political ideology, but a technological platform, offering a viable path of development for both mature democracies and authoritarian regimes.
It does not impose values—it provides a tool that works in any political system.
For Democratic Countries: An Answer to the Legitimacy Crisis
Modern representative democracy has exhausted itself.
Elections every few years, the influence of party structures and lobbying have led to the alienation of citizens from power.
Society increasingly feels: there is a voice, but there is no influence.
QazLike is not destruction, but a democracy upgrade.
It is Democracy 2.0, where all basic freedoms are preserved, but direct participation, technological transparency, and protection from corruption are added.
This is not a revolution, but an evolutionary response to society's demand for real, not ritual, control over the state.
For Authoritarian Countries: A Historic Chance for Peaceful Transformation
For the first time in history, a model appears that allows for systemic reforms without violence and chaos.
QazLike creates a common material interest for all—citizens, the army, and security forces.
When the military and police see that they and their families become shareholders of the country, receiving direct dividends, their loyalty naturally shifts from protecting a narrow group in power to protecting the interests of the entire society.
Thus, the main source of revolutions is eliminated—the conflict between the people and the security structures that previously supported the state apparatus.
The Inevitability Effect
The QazLike model possesses killer appeal.
It speaks a universal language—of personal benefit, justice, and transparency.
Once the idea becomes known to society, an irresistible demand for change arises.
Politicians supporting the transition to QazLike inevitably gain the trust and support of the majority.
The transition to QazLike becomes not an act of political struggle, but the embodiment of collective common sense.
This is the technologically predetermined future of state governance.
